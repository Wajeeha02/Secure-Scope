Our purpose here at Secure Scope

The breakdown of our core initiative at Secure Scope is shown below.

Approximately 400 SMEs round the globe which contribute to 60% to 70% of employment globally

The foundation of economies all around the world is made up of the 400 million or so SMEs. They account for about 95% of businesses and 60% to 70% of employment globally, making them the primary source of job generation. Providing free of cost security services to 95% of businesses means protecting more than half of global economy.

Increase in Cyber Attacks on Small Businesses by 424% as of 2017 and 43% of Cyber Atacks target small businesses.

These alrming numbers indicate we need to take adequate actions by supporting SMEs which we at Secure Scope provide professionaly at no cost.

Only 49% of the total 88% of companies who face Cyber threats are aware of importance of CyberSecurity for their businesses.

The 5 of the Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations we are focused upon.

Goal 8:

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Our solution to provide cybersecurity services to small and medium-sized businesses helps create a secure and stable business environment, which can promote economic growth and job creation, contributing to the goal of decent work and economic growth. By providing businesses with the necessary tools and knowledge to protect themselves against cyber threats, we can enable them to operate more efficiently and effectively, resulting in sustainable economic growth.

Goal 9:

Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

Secure Scope can accelerate innovation and promote the development of new technologies by promoting the adoption of secure and resilient digital infrastructure. By providing cybersecurity services to businesses,we facilitate the growth of a sustainable and inclusive economy, in line with the goal of industry, innovation and infrastructure.

Goal 10:

Reduced Inequalities

Secure Scope helps reduce inequalities by providing free cybersecurity services to middle and low-level businesses. By offering essential business resources and services to businesses that may not have had access to them previously, we promote access to essential resources and services, contributing to the goal of reduced inequalities.

Goal 16:

Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Secyre Scope promotes cybersecurity best practices and standards, contributing to the creation of more secure and stable digital environments. This promotes the rule of law and reduce the risk of cybercrime and other digital threats, aligning with the goal of peace, justice and strong institutions.

Goal 17:

Partnerships

Secure Scope fosters partnerships and collaborations between businesses, governments and other stakeholders. By promoting knowledge sharing, cooperation, and innovation, we achieve sustainable development in line with the goal of partnerships for the goals.
